A tutorial for Minecraft

Minecraft can be a very challenging game. This website is designed to help you on your journey through it and demolish your friends or prove to them how good you are.

Starting off with the basics

  1. In your new survival world, go to a tree to get some wood. Wood is an essential crafting material, for early game necessities such as tooks and a bed. After that, make a wooden pickaxe, mine for stone and get 16. This will be enough to get a stone sword, axe, pickaxe, and a furnace where you can cook your food. The other two, a shovel and hoe are not needed.

  2. If you still have daylight, find some sheep and get their wool to make a bed and survive the first night by sleeping in it. It is not necessary to make a house or base as in the beginning of the game, you will scavenge for better gear and then towards the middle game you would be more sedentary.

  3. Now find a cave that looks like it has potential for ores such as iron and diamond. Diamond is not needed, but if you can ever find some, that is amazing, and you should capitalize off of it. If you do see gold, grab some because that will be important for trading with piglins in the nether, for later.

  4. Now you can start building your house, preferably in a plains biome near a lake, village, and forest which can provide great materials.

  5. Make sure to keep in mind of making utilities like golden apples, a bow with arrows, and stacks of blocks from trips mining, which will be useful to stack up in the End. Make sure to have a water bucket at all times to break fall damage.

  11. When the dragon perches, use your sword to hit its head.

  12. After enough time of this, great job, you have beaten the game.

Building

Picking a theme

Think about a them you want to pick. My personal favorite themes to build are old-fashioned houses and interiors.

Color Palette

Pick a color scheme that would look nice on your build and stick to it. You may involve blocks with different textures. If your build was a run down villa, you might want to have sandstone and cracked sandstone.

Outline

Before you start your build, make an outline in a specific block, if you're in creativ use different kinds of wool.

Depth

You use depth to not make your build look samey. You add different textures.

Crafting

Tools

In order to craft tools, you have to have sticks and whatever material you want the tools to be made of.

Ex. Diamond Sword = 2 diamonds and one stick

Armor

In order to craft tools, you have to put your material for the armor you want to make to make the different pieces of the full armor.

For armour in general, you would need 24 of that material.

Minecraft Facts

  1. In the older versions of Minecraft. the world border, millions of blocks away from spawn was deformed. It is iconic of the early versions of Minceraft. They are called the Farlands.
  2. The hostile mob, the creeper, was created because it was supposed to be a pig, but came out wrong when a developer coded it. They decided to keep it as a monster.
  3. To fight mobs better, or players, don't spam click. Wait for the meter in the middle of your screen near the crosshair to be charged up, indicating that you can now make a more powerful hit.
  4. In the Nether dimension, you can find structures called bastions, and get gold to trade with piglins for ender pearls. Bastions are dangerous, high risk high reward.
  5. The original enderman had green eyes.
